# Imvidia-Electronics
"Powering Lives, at a Better Price."

Welcome to ImVidia! Founded in 2015 as a local business specialising in kitchen equipments, it later rebranded into ImVidia Electronics after recognising the anti-consumerism market that is the market of electronic appliances, stepping up to bridge the gap between price and quality. Since then, ImVidia has expanded its product range and evolved into a trusted brand commited to enhancing everyday comfort and improving lives.

This Repository is for ImVidia's upcoming Dynamic Web Application.
